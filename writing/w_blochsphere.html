Visualising Mixed States on the Bloch Sphere [WIP]

Mixed states in quantum mechanics are convex combinations of pure states. Pure states blue and red have a magnitude of 1 on the Bloch sphere below. The resulting green mixed state can be represented by a vector with magnitude less than 1 on the Bloch sphere!

A mixed state of 3 pure states on the Bloch Sphere exists somewhere on the 2D convex hull defined by the 3 pure state vertices. Is the convex hull of N pure states in general N-1 dimensional? The convex hull of a finite set of points like this is called a convex polytope.

We can very quickly think of some strange states using this visualisation. The mixed state of the red and blue vectors below with equal weights q is in fact the zero vector! This is true of any two anti-parallel vectors on the Bloch Sphere.
